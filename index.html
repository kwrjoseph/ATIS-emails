Dear Valued Customer,

We are pleased to share some exciting news: Vermont Elevator Inspection
Services (VEIS) has joined ATIS
(https://atis.com/?utm_source=organic&utm_medium=mcae&utm_campaign=24q4-us-east-veisacquisition),
a premier provider of elevator and escalator safety inspections, consulting
and managed services. This acquisition expands our collective presence in
Vermont and enhances our capacity to deliver quality inspection services
across the region.

With the support of ATIS, VEIS is better positioned to meet the growing
demand for elevator and lift inspection services throughout the state, and
ATIS is pleased to offer enhanced consulting services to all our Vermont
customers.

What does this mean for you? You will continue to receive the same
high-quality elevator, lift and escalator inspection services you expect.
-The VEIS team will remain intact as part of ATIS. Familiar faces will
continue to support and coordinate your inspections from our local office.
-You will also benefit from expanded customer service and administrative
support, a larger team of best-in-class, state-certified inspectors, and
improved service delivery.
-The trusted team you know will continue to serve you, with a commitment to
providing the same—or even better—level of service.

 Please review the Certificate of Insurance (COI) and W9 on our website
(https://atis.com/w9-cois?utm_source=organic&utm_medium=mcae&utm_campaign=24q4-us-east-veisacquisition).

Access CoI and W9:
https://atis.com/w9-cois?utm_source=organic&utm_medium=mcae&utm_campaign=24q4-us-east-veisacquisition

If you require your company to be named on the COI as a certificate holder,
please provide that requested information to insurance@atis.com
(mailto:insurance@atis.com).

ATIS’s Vermont team is here to serve you, and your current customer
service contacts will remain available. Please feel free to reach out with
any questions or concerns. We look forward to delivering a superior
customer experience, prioritizing your safety, and maintaining integrity
and impartiality. Thank you for your business; we look forward to serving
you in the future.
